## First comment (maker's intro)
> Hi everyone, maker here 👋
>
> Ubuntu Zombie turns a supported Ubuntu Desktop LTS machine into a computer
> that can administer itself. It installs a dedicated, root-capable Linux
> account as the operating identity of an AI Systems Administrator. You chat
> with it privately on 127.0.0.1, ask in plain English, and it proposes the
> exact commands — which only run after you approve. Everything is audit-logged
> and reversible.
>
> It's built around keeping you in control: a local policy gate before any
> privileged action, localhost-only services, key-only SSH with root disabled,
> opt-in Tailscale for remote access, and a kill switch you hold (rotate the
> key, remove the SSH key, or `uninstall`). It's a transparent bash installer —
> inspect every line.
>
> It's MIT-licensed and free; you bring your own LLM provider key. I'd love
> feedback, especially on the trust model (SECURITY.md). AMA!
