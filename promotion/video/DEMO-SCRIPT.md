## Production notes
- Use a clean throwaway VM so nothing sensitive appears on screen.
- Redact any API keys, hostnames, or IPs in post.
- Keep the orchid (#AC43D9) as the only accent colour in titles/captions.
- No music with a "scary AI" vibe — keep it calm and neutral.
- Export a 15–20s cut-down (steps 1, 3–5, 9) for social autoplay.

## Captions / accessibility
- Burn in captions; most social views are muted.
- Provide an `.srt` and a text transcript alongside the upload.
